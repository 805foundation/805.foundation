About Us

The Foundation

805 Foundation believes in investing in the younger generation. We believe that Universities or other educational institutions are not enough. Students need a way to develop practical experience to expand their knowledge beyond the typical classroom setting. We have helped students expand their horizons and gain new skills through work-based learning.

Stories Worth Telling

805 Productions focuses on the entertainment industry producing shortfilms which are entered in local film festivals, capturing documentaries about San Diego locals, businessmen, or public figures, and creating promotional videos for companies, brands, or organizations. In the future, we plan on expanding towards podcasts, gaming, music, and dance.

Students As A Service

805 DevOps is the software division that specializes in web, app, and game development creating portfolio websites, eccomerce websites, and more. We also produce in-house projects such as games or apps that we have distributed free for consumer use.

The Initiative

Project 619 is how we give back to the community. We are a registered student organization at San Diego State University that actively participates in several community service events, hosts community based, family friendly events and programs free for all, and annually we choose to focus on one large city project to leave a lasting impact in the city of San Diego.
