Perks and Benefits

Freedom of creativity
We encourage our interns to take the initiative to create their own events, programs, products and services. Our interns work at full autonomy and have every opportunity to choose what type of projects they would like to work on. Typically they choose porjects they know will help them advance them in their professional field of endeavors.
Network with professionals
Our teams actively participate in outside events such as film festivals, hackathons, and pitch competitions where the have multiple opportunities to meet like-minded peers and professionals in their respective industires.
Mentorship for progress
Learning about our fields of interest in class and through personal projects are great ways of improving our skills and expanding on our knowledge. However, nothing is as good as having a mentor who has experience going down the same path we are moving towards. It's invaluable to have mentor who can answer specific questions, give advice, and update us on the current trends in the industry.
Culture of entrepreneurship
Many of have ideas of events, programs, products or services we would like to share with the world. Creating an entirely new club and dealing with univsersity banking or regulations get complicated. Use our back-end to launch your startup. As a non-profit organization, a recognized 501 (c)(3), we are able to provide students with software, mentors, interns, banking, etc.
Unpaid Intern Status
A lot of students today build their own projects or products. Why not do the things you love to do while building your resume?
Fun and collaborative experience
Work with a diverse group of people who can pitch ideas, work with you on your projects, and have fun doing it!
